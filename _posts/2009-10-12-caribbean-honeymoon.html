We know that weddings are special occasions and newlyweds always look for right honeymoon destination to get prefect setting of their new life. But choosing the right honeymoon destination is not an easy task, you need to consider carefully as such a joyous occasion needs to have a grand finale. Probably Caribbean islands are the most appealing & attractive honeymoon destinations for most of the Grooms.

A Caribbean honeymoon gives you chance to travel a spot of the world where the calm seas and wonderful beaches add the pleasure in your wedding life. And if you are going to have honeymoon in Caribbean island then there are over 7000 Caribbean Islands you can choose from. Each Island has its own cultural and natural flavor that provides romantic appeal, beaches and attraction for everyone. Each island is placed with at least one all inclusive resort that appeals for attractive and wide range of accommodations options.

So you are ready to have a romantic honeymoon in Caribbean Island but you have concerns regarding budget that you have to spend to have romantic and appealing start of newly married life. Then there are lots of options you have under your radar that suits best your pocket. Almost all of the tour operators in any part of world provide different type of Caribbean Honeymoon packages including royal Caribbean honeymoon packages, luxury Caribbean Honeymoon packages, budget Caribbean Honeymoon packages and all inclusive Caribbean Honeymoon packages.

Each of these packages includes all the ingredients that make your Caribbean vacation an unforgettable vacation. These packages include a perfect Caribbean Honeymoon destination, a lovely Resort, Cruises and adventurous sports those are enough to make your honeymoon a best honeymoon. \nBut if you want to have an exotic Caribbean honeymoon experience then Jamaica is the place where you need to go. Because Jamaica is very big island with large cities around it that offer you each and everything according to your need including shopping, clubbing, bars, restaurants and amusement parks. In Jamaica you also find abundance of all inclusive resorts and villas that make your honeymoon planning much simpler.

If Jamaica is not best match as you are looking for then you could go for uniquely Lush Island that offers romance with style named St. Lucia. It is the place once you spent time here then it will always be held in your heart. Here, at St. Lucia you have many beaches, plantations or rain forest to enjoy nature beauty at its best. Here you have wide range of resorts or villas that give romantic appeal and the popular Anse Chastanet which boast stunning views of the Piton Mountains. But the most famous and romantic spot in St. Lucia is Almond Morgan Bay that welcomes couples, singles and families with a wide view of ocean. Almond Morgan Bay resort has four gourmet restaurants giving choice of French 'Creole' cuisine, Caribbean/Asian fusion, a continental restaurant and seafood restaurant.
