The latest Netcraft Web Server Survey is out and the Apache Web Server increased its lead in that market with a gain of 2.09% bringing its total 'market share' to around 62.53%.

IIS(20.38%) and IPlanet(7.0%) each dipped about .7% and .5% respectivly.

Apache gained over a million sites in the month of June to bring it past the 10 million site mark. Along with the Bea WebLogic server was the only server to gain market share this month.
