This edition will put mainframe-like transaction handling into the Web system providing high performance and high data " --- IBM plans to ship the Enterprise Edition of its WebSphere product in September. This edition will put mainframe-like transaction handling into the Web system providing high performance and high data integrity. WebSphere will make it easier to integrate a Web front-end with legacy back-end systems like mainframes.

Initial pricing is $35,000 per processor and $7,500 per developer. It will be available for NT, AIX, and Solaris.
