To get some excitement going about the upcoming release of Netscape 6 and to get more developers interested in the new product, Netscape is sponsoring a themes contest.

The new release of Netscape, based on the Mozilla project, has technology built in that allows for the browser and its components to be 'skinable'. You can see some examples at Mozillazine.org.

Prizes include a Philips Plasma TV(yummy), a Sony Digital Camera, and an Apple iBook for the top three places. Ten honorable mentions will recieve Diamond Rio MP3 players as well. If you're interested, find out how to get started creating themes. Hit the homepage for more details, rules, etc..
