The Cubes which appear next to a name are used to specify how many articles that member has written. The more contributions a member makes, the more cubes they get! The breakdown is presented below:

No. of Articles
Written		The Cubes
None yet.
1 to 5
6 to 10
11 to 15
16 to 20
21 to 30
31 to 45
46 to 65
66 to 100
101 to 150
151 to 200
200 +
