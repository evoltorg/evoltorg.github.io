Many people believe that evolt.org is run by a single individual or a small group of technocrats. The reality of this is that it is the members of evolt.org that run evolt.org. Whether it is participating in discussions on our mailing list, adding browsers to the browser archive, contributing articles to thesite or building the features that will power the next incarnation of evolt.org, the members are running the show.

However, as with any organization, there is a group of people who must harness all the great work of evolt.org's members and we do so by having a number of mailing lists to churn through the future direction and day-to-day operations that a swank organisation like evolt.org entails.

Evolt.org is run by evolt.org members from Australia, Austria, Canada, Chile, England, Iceland, India, New Zealand, Scotland, and all over the US. Not one of us is compensated monetarily for our efforts and contributions, and we continue in the spirit of community to make evolt.org as successful as possible.

Because evolt.org is becoming a complex beast, and to let people work on areas they're most interested in, evolt's admin work is split between five working groups:
