--- layout: post permalink: node/312 --- Telewest and MSN have announced the intention to roll out a broadband network in 2000.

\nMSN are to provide a range of interactive content, while Telewest, the UK's largest cable provider, will provide the connectivity via cable modems.

\nNo prices have been announced, but it seems likely to be substantially better value than BT announced for their ADSL rollout.
