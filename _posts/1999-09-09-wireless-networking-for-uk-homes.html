--- layout: post permalink: node/424 --- British Telecom is to roll-out a wireless networking technology aimed at home users and small businesses.

\nFrom the start of next year, users will be able to link laptops, PCs and any other digital devices without resorting to re-cabling their homes and offices.

\nThe system was developed by a BT-funded US start-up, Home Wireless Networks. Each device is fitted with a transmitter/receiver, which then allows it to communicate with a main controller or base station. At the heart of the system is a Wireless Gateway, which will support international standards including ADSL and ISDN.

\nBT estimates the technology will generate sales in excess of #35m in its first year, and predicts that more than 10 million users will sign up by 2004.
