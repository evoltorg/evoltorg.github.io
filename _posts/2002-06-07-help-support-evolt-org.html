Why give?

evolt.org is an all-volunteer organization that relies on \nthe contributions of its members to operate and function. Thanks in part to the \ngenerous commitment of the web community, evolt.org provides renowned resources \nand information without advertisements. \nHere is a list of some of the services we provide:

    \n
  • The Browser Archive
    \n The largest browser archive available anywhere!
    • \n
  • Articles
    \n Articles, tutorials and news items written by our members and available free \n to any visitor.
    • \n
  • List Servs
    \n Numerous email lists to help disseminate knowledge to the web development \n community, and to help run evolt.org.

  • The Directory Project
    \n A searchable directory containing links covering web design and development.
    • \n

The opportunities and services we provide are an increasing drain on \nevolt.org's limited finances. Bandwidth costs are our biggest expenditure; we \ncurrently transfer 250 GB/month, and that usage is growing. Hence, the \nneed to ask for your help.

\n

Financial donations

evolt.org is currently able to take donations online using PayPal, or by \nsnail mail if you prefer. You can donate via PayPal in US dollars no matter what \nyour home currency.

Make an automated monthly donation

Suport evolt.org every month using PayPal subscriptions.  (Please \nnote: Due to PayPal transaction fees, we only offer the monthly online donation option \nin amounts of $10 US or more. If you'd like to give less, we appreciate your \nfunding and encourage you to use the #one">next method.)

\n
\n

You can cancel your subscription at any time using your PayPal account. If \nyou don't have a PayPal account, it's fast and easy to sign up during the \nregistration process.

Make a one-time donation

Google Ads in the Browser Archive

We have begun running Google AdSense ads within the Browser Archive.

If you'd like, you can now advertise exclusively on evolt.org! You can use this link to sign up for a Google AdWords account and target ads to browsers.evolt.org.

Placing an ad on evolt.org benefits both you (if you're trying to reach a high-traffic, high-quality audience of web designers, developers, hobbyists and professionals) and evolt.org too, because site-targeted ads generate more revenue for the care, feeding and hosting of evolt.org.

If you see any ads appear which are inappropriate, or which appear to interfere with your access to the Browser Archive, please let us know as soon as possible.

Knowledge donations

We welcome many types of contributions, and recognise that not all of them \nwill be tangible. We value the evolt.org community members who donate their \ntime, skills, opinions and expertise to our various list servers, articles, and \nadministration and management demands. While evolt.org's financial needs are \ngreat, there is no evolt without those contributions.

Thanks!

If you have questions, please contact \ngive@evolt.org. Regardless of what or how much you \nchoose to contribute to evolt.org, it is sincerely appreciated.
