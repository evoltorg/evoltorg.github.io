Qualcomm, the makers of the popular Eudora mail client recently announced they plan on releasing the software for free.

According to the press release, the software will have three different operating modes, sponsored, paid, and light. The full features of the software will be unlocked when one is using it in either 'paid' or 'sponsored' mode, while light mode will remain as it currenlty is.
