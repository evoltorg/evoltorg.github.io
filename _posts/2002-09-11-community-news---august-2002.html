Another month gone, another stack of accomplishments by the\nevolt.org community. As a reminder, this is open to anyone in the\ncommunity, so if you missed a chance to toot your horn, add a\ncomment, and watch for the announcement for next month's news.

evolt.org

At the beginning of August we successfully moved all (5) of\nevolt.org's servers from their old home at Starkmedia to Ron Dorman's facility's CSI. Thanks to everyone that helped in the move and\nthe subsequent setup and testing. Special thanks go to Dan Cody\nwho has setup, hosted, and maintained the servers for so long and who\nprepared them for the move; to Matt Warden who drove the 900\nmiles between Milwaukee and Altavista but also the 800 miles from his\nhome to Milwaukee in order to transport the servers; and to Ron Dorman\nfor the current hosting and maintenance of the servers and the large\namount of setup that he and Dan went through to get them all in\nworking order. Finally thanks to all of the members that dontated\nmoney in order to fund the move. It is very much appreciated.

The second edition of Information Architecture for the World Wide Web is\nout and features, among other sites, our own evolt.org community. Our\nown Javier Velasco was\nlisted in the acknowledgements.\nCongratulations to all that were involved in the project.

Finally, the first of the browser archive has its first mirror site donated by the evolt.org.uk boys.
