Another month gone, another stack of accomplishments by the

evolt.org community. As a reminder, this is open to anyone in the

community, so if you missed a chance to href="http://evolt.org/contact/index.html">toot your horn, add a

comment, and watch for the announcement for next month's news.

evolt.org

At the beginning of August we successfully moved all (5) of

evolt.org's servers from their old home at href="http://www.starkmedia.com" title="link to Starkmedia"

target="_new">Starkmedia to Ron Dorman's facility's href="http://www.csi1st.net/" title="link to CSI 1st"

target="_new">CSI. Thanks to everyone that helped in the move and

the subsequent setup and testing. Special thanks go to href="http://www.five2one.org/" title="djc" target="_new">Dan Cody

who has setup, hosted, and maintained the servers for so long and who

prepared them for the move; to title="Matt Warden" target="_new">Matt Warden who drove the 900

miles between Milwaukee and Altavista but also the 800 miles from his

home to Milwaukee in order to transport the servers; and to Ron Dorman

for the current hosting and maintenance of the servers and the large

amount of setup that he and Dan went through to get them all in

working order. Finally thanks to all of the members that dontated

money in order to fund the move. It is very much appreciated.

The second edition of href="http://www.amazon.com/exec/obidos/ASIN/0596000359/evoltorg/104-4413480-3081519"

target="_new">Information Architecture for the World Wide Web is

out and features, among other sites, our own evolt.org community. Our

own Javier Velasco was

listed in the href="http://members.evolt.org/mantruc/digicam/polar_bear_ack_small.jpg">acknowledgements.

Congratulations to all that were involved in the project.

Finally, the first of the browser archive has its first mirror site donated by the evolt.org.uk boys.

fresh opportunities relevant to all of us. Submitted items from month

to month may be of varying relevance to others depending on the number

more about yourself, and learn more about the people that make up href="/article/roundup/27/18210/index.html" title="The evolt.org

Monthly Recap">the evolt.org Monthly Recap introductory

article.

If you're proud of it, send it

in. Don't be shy.
