The development work has been mostly done by Netscape engineers with little involvement from outside help. According to Sun Microsystem's Software Products and Platorm Division president, Alan Baratz, "I'm not sure Mozilla.org is working all that well." Sun is looking into other options for the future development of the browser. However, Baratz predicts that Communicator 5.0 will ship in December regardless.
