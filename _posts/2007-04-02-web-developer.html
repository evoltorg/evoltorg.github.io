Seamless Marketing and Communications Ltd. (www.seamless-marketing.co.uk) is looking for a Web developer who has a minimum of 6 months commercial experience to work at our offices in Bradford, West Yorkshire. Experience/understanding of some of the following would be desirable:

  • Skills in XHTML, CSS, XML, ASP, PHP, JavaScript
  • Accessibility requirements for Disability Discrimination Act
  • Database Integration to MS-SQL

Please send your cv, links to examples of your work and covering letter to ask@seamless-marketing.co.uk
