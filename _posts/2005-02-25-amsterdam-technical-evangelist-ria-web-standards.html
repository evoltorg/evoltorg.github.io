Backbase is an innovative software company that develops software for Rich Internet Applications based on Internet Standards. We are looking for a Technical Evangelist who can get developers worldwide excited about Backbase. You will develop demos, code samples and sample applications, which will stretch the limits of the technology. You will develop best practices and spread the knowledge both externally and internally within Backbase. You will grow a community of developers and architects, and occasionally some more business-oriented users. You will do Backbase technical presentations on conferences. Using your product and market expertise, you are closely involved with Product Management and R&D in the development of specifications for future product releases.

Requirements

  • Excellent communication skills

  • Expert knowledge of CSS, (X)HTML, JavaScript and web standards

  • Solid background as a software developer, with good knowledge of advanced system architecture and design patterns

  • Good conceptual skills

  • A drive to innovate and the motivation to continuously upgrade your skills

  • Work experience in a similar role is a plus

  • Work permit for the Netherlands

About Backbase

Backbase is an innovative and ambitious software company, which is regarded as one of Europe's most promising software startups. The company is well-managed, and has been profitable from day one. Currently there are 30+ employees. Backbase develops products in the area of Rich Internet Applications (RIAs). The Backbase Rich User Interface suite allows developers to quickly develop rich and responsive web-based user interfaces that integrate well with any type of back-end system. The reliance on open standards and support for all major web browsers without a plug-in makes Backbase unique in the world. For more information, please visit www.backbase.com/jobs.
