Mastered ColdFusion? Need to manage a content-heavy site? You've heard of Allaire's Übertool, Spectra - it's a CF-based app which makes content management a snap. But, it's a bit more work to develop for. The Allaire site has next to no documentation (other than info about the very, very expensive training course). So how do you learn?

Well a nice chap by the name of Scott rides to the rescue. He's putting together a step-by-step guide to putting together a Spectra application. As the jazz tv presenter would have said, "Nice!"
