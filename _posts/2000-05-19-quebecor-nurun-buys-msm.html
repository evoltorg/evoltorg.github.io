The official press release:

NURUN AND ANTARTICA GROUP ACQUIRE MSM INTERACTIVE\n First step in Latin American development strategy

SANTIAGO, CHILE, May 19, 2000

\n In the presence of the Prime Minister of Quebec. Mr. Lucien Bouchard, in Santiago as part of\n Quebec Mission, nurun Inc., a subsidiary of the Quebecor Inc. group and formerly known as\n Informission Group Inc., and the Antartica Group announced the first acquisition resulting from\n their recent strategic partnership. nurun has entered into an agreement together with\n Antartica Group to acquire a controlling interest in MSM Interactive S.A., an e-commerce and\n Web integration company based in Santiago, Chile.

MSM Interactive (www.msm.cl) comprises a team of 40 designers, programmers and marketing\n experts specializing in Web-related solutions. The company's services span Web site design\n and development, e-commerce, including Market Place solutions, Web-based training and\n wireless Internet. Current clients include BellSouth Chile, Concha Y Toro, Ericsson, Sheraton\n San Cristobal, Citibank and Banco Sud Americano. MSM Interactive is known as one of the\n largest independent new media agencies in Chile.

"We are very excited about having a leader like MSM Interactive to spearhead our Latin\n American expansion," said Jacques Malo, President and CEO of nurun Inc. "They are a dynamic\n organization that is on top of the latest Web trends and solutions. They have the elements of\n a winning Web integrator and are a perfect fit with nurun's focus on the seamless convergence\n of strategy, marketing, design and technology."

"Having access to nurun's expertise and international network as well as Antartica's network of\n clients and management resources will put some real air under our wings," said Gonzalo Alonso,\n Co-founder and General Manager of MSM Interactive. "We'll now be part of a team that\n stretches across the Americas and Europe. "

"E-Commerce and multimedia products are growing at a dramatic pace, offering to Quebec\n based companies business opportunities in their home market as well as in the global market\n place, " declared the Prime Minister of Quebec.

The transaction is subject to completing legal purchase documents including obtaining required\n approval from regulatory authorities.

nurun Inc. (Formerly Informission Group Inc. ; TSE: IFN), a subsidiary of the Quebecor Inc.\n group, is an information technology company providing e-commerce, Web integration and\n automated publishing solutions for corporate and public sector clients in North America, Europe\n and select locations around the world. nurun employs more than 1000 professionals in its\n various offices in Montreal, Quebec, Toronto, Ottawa, Seattle, New York, Boston, Chicago and\n Paris.

Founded in Santiago in 1998, MSM Interactive (www.msm.cl) is a 2nd generation Web\n Integrator that services its clients with horizontal and vertical solutions for those enterprises\n that are participating in the new digital economy of e-business, field in which has received\n national and foreign recognition, establishing important joint ventures.v
