The Web Standards Project is taking issue with the lack of standards support in Microsoft's newly released Internet Explorer 5.5 Windows Edition,\n and lambasting the company for an "arrogant" break with standards.

\n

\nIn the announcement released today, the WSP expresses frustration with whats become a nightmare for developers in terms of re-writing code for different versions of IE, not to mention the coding differences between IE on different platforms.\n
