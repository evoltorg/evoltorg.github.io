We're getting organized

\n

Behind the scenes, evolt.org members have developed five groups, plus two \n other bodies, to help organize and run evolt.org. The five groups and their \n purposes are:

\n
    \n
  • Content
    \n Responsible, for editing and approving articles which appear on www.evolt.org, \n responding to emails that come through the site and day-to-day management \n of the evolt mailing lists.
    • \n
  • Development
    \n Responsible for the design and implementation of various projects.
    • \n
  • Finance
    \n Responsible for handling the money side of evolt.org. Fundraising efforts \n are jointly made with...
    • \n
  • Marketing
    \n Responsible for building and maintaining the evolt.org brand, and for managing \n all external relationships.
    • \n
  • Sysadmin
    \n Responsible for administering the evolt.org servers.
    • \n
\n

The Steering Committee, being organized now, will be made \n up of two members from each of the 5 groups. Its purpose is to help set goals \n for the groups, monitor progress, and solve disputes—should they arise—amongst \n the groups.

\n

TheForum is a catch-all list for general communication between \n the different evolt.org committees, and for discussing non-committee-specific \n issues. Anyone can join \n TheForum, and we encourage your participation. If you have ideas for evolt.org, \n or can contribute even a little bit of time and energy into helping us thrive, \n we'd love to have you!
