Greetings and welcome! We've recently changed servers for the evolt.org website to provide better service(basically we were starting to get killed on our other machine :)

Not only did we move servers, but also to a different operating system(Linux), Database(Oracle), and version of Cold Fusion(4.5) - so even though we tested everything out, there might be some problems.
