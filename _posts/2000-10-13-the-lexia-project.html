Have you ever said to yourself "this is stupid" while reading long \n text on your computer screen? We all know that it's not the best way to read, \n but what else can we do?

\n

Today we receieved an invitation to comment and join the efforts of a team \n that's trying to solve this problem.

\n
\n

The lexiaProject Public GUI \n Initiative is a collaborative Open Content License project to create a better \n graphical user interface (GUI) for reading extended texts on a computer screen. \n The project uses a new technique, called vertical serial text presentation \n (VSTP) in combination with a unique interface widget to accommodate rapid, \n hands-free reading.
