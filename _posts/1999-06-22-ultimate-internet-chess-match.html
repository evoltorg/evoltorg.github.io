--- layout: post permalink: node/176 --- Think you can beat Kasparov? Have a look at his latest challenge.

\nIn a bold move, Kasparov challenges the world to beat him at chess. He will play a move, then four grandmasters will suggest counter-moves. The world will then vote online on which is to be played. Of course, strategy goes out the window on both sides, but it should be a larf.
