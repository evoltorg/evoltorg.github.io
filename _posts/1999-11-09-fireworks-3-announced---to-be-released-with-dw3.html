--- layout: post permalink: node/557 --- Macromedia have announced that Fireworks3 is to be released with Dreamweaver3 in an integrated studio suite.

\nAmongst the new features of Fireworks are:

  • Extended Javascripting
  • Export to Flash
  • Enhanced stylesheets
  • A new symbols library

\nThe suite, to be released later this year, will strongly feature the integration between the two products, and is currently expected to have upgrade prices from $199.
