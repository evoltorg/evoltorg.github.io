--- layout: post permalink: node/606 --- TheStandard.com is reporting that Palestinians now have their own top-level domain: .ps, after help from Ghassan Z. Qadah (senior adviser for technical affairs to the Palestinian Authority), and with the assistance of the United Nations, the Internet Assigned Numbers Authority, and the International Standards Organization.

You can read TheStandard.com's article here.

No information was given on how, or if, people will be able to register .ps domains.
