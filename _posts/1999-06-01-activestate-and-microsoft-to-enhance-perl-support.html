\nActiveState and Microsoft have just done up a 3-year contract for Perl Open Source development and support for Windows.\n

\n

\nActiveState will add features such as full Unicode support for Windows. In addition, what ActiveState refers to as a "significant amount of the development effort" will be released as open source code.\n

\n

\nBetas of the next release are scheduled for August 1999.\n

\n

\nYou can see the press release on the ActiveState site at:\nhttp://www.activestate.com/press/releases/Microsoft.htm\n
