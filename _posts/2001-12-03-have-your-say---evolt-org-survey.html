Soliciting feedback

After almost three years building a web development community, evolt.org is holding its first-ever survey. What features do you most enjoy?\nWhat features do you never use? Do you have a beef you want to express, but don't know who to contact? \nWhat articles interest you the most on the main evolt.org web site? Do you have ideas and/or suggestions on new projects for evolt.org? \nNow's the time... take the survey and let your opinions be known! We'd like to get as much feedback as possible, so feel free to \nforward this on to web development co-workers, friends, websites, and lists!

Take the survey now!

Who's going to review the results?

All responses will be read by real live people, discussed, debated, and given as much consideration as you take in providing them. \nA group from theforum will assess the data. Issues and concerns raised by the survey will be discussed and debated on theforum, as well. \nResults will be tabulated and a summary article posted in this category.

Thanks for hosting

The evolt.org survey is graciously being hosted on Survey Monkey. Many thanks to Ryan Finley.
