Another couple of months gone, another stack of accomplishments by the

evolt.org community. As a reminder, this is open to anyone in the

community, so if you missed a chance to href="http://evolt.org/contact/index.html">toot your horn, add a

comment, and watch for the announcement for next month's news.

evolt.org

A new supporter has stepped forward to help support evolt.org.

To assist us in defraying our operating costs, NetWorth Enterprises,

Inc. is offering an affiliate hosting program to evolt.org members.

For every member who chooses to host one or more web sites with

NetWorth's hosting service, evolt.org will receive 10% of the monthly

hosting fee.

If you're looking for a hosting company, please consider NetWorth's

hosting service. NetWorth will be changing the name and enhancing

services, but in the

meantime, the temporary domain (ez-profits.com) will

help evolt.org right now.

If you want to know more about the connection between NetWorth and

evolt.org, please read this explanation.

Evoltspottings

All over the web, people are looking to see what evolters have to

say, and sometimes they point their users back to the source. Here are

some of the pointers, all noticed this month:

  • Evolt.org mailing lists were mentioned on the Acknowledgements page in Christina Wodtke's recently released book "Information Architecture: Blueprints for the Web".

  • Shirley Singh's article on volunteerism and the Internet mentions evolt.org and quotes some of its members in her article at rediff.com See the second part in particular.

  • Tara Clevland's article on Accessibility Laws in Canada was mentioned on the ACM's CHI-WEB mailing list.

Thanks to the many people who have taken the time to cite evolt.org

in their own work

Last year, I won second place is website design (thanks to thelist for making me wanting to learn more about web "design" as opposed to web "coding"). This year, I got honorable mention in C++. Submissions

fresh opportunities relevant to all of us. Submitted items from month

to month may be of varying relevance to others depending on the number

more about yourself, and learn more about the people that make up href="/article/roundup/27/18210/index.html" title="The evolt.org

Monthly Recap">the evolt.org Monthly Recap introductory
