Web analytics studies the behavior of website visitors. It especially refers to he use of data collected from a web site to determine which aspects of the website work to bring on line business objectives to life.

Therefore, web analytics is getting more importance in the world of on line marketing. Web analytics tells you what your visitors are really interested in, what keywords people use to find you on the search engines, how many people clicked through from their recent e-mail campaign and also lets you know the ROI (return on investment) on their marketing dollars. Therefore, your on line marketing can get a leg up.

There are so many other functions of the web analytics that can help you to improvise and get visitors. It can tell you whether you have dead content. You can change this, organize the images on your site, make the site more attractive by adding colors or links that increase the relevancy of your keywords and so many other things. It tells you where your visitors are coming from so that you know what your target should be or how to expand on the existing market that you are getting. You can make available translation services to make the whole world understand what you are trying to say. This helps in reaching a wider audience.

In short web analytics helps you to analyze the performance of your website so that you can make necessary changes and enhancements in the website. Web analytics can also tell you what techniques you should use like search engine optimization (SEO), link buying/renting and many other applications that will get you a high ranking in the search engine results. This way you can reach an even wider audience because you website will be listed as one of the top websites pertaining to your product.

It has been observed that a pattern emerges in the users of web analytics. These companies have made profit and have experienced faster growth. When web analytics are properly used, they can answer important business questions related to advertising investments, market segmentation and web design. Web analytics gives you the SWOT analysis ,that is, your strengths, weaknesses, opportunities and threats. This helps you to make a site that makes on line marketing a success.
