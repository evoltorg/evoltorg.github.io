A reciprocal link is a joint link between two substances or objects, frequently between two websites in order to guarantee shared traffic. For example Ali and Babar have websites. If Babar’s website relates to Ali's website, and Ali's website relates to Babar's website, the websites are reciprocally linked. Reciprocal linking process is done in order to increase the page rank of website. Reciprocal linking between websites grew to be an central piece of the Search Engine Optimization process
